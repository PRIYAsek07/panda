Giant Panda - Xing Qiu and Yi Lan - Adelaide Zoo
The term "panda" most commonly refers to the giant panda, a large, black and white bear native to China. They are known for their distinctive black and white fur, and their dependence on bamboo for food. However, "panda" can also refer to the red panda, a smaller, reddish-brown mammal found in the Eastern Himalayas.

Top 5 Facts About Pandas - WWF

  • They have a pseudo thumb for grasping bamboo
  • Pandas mainly eat bamboo
  • Pandas spend most of their day eating
  • They are born pink and blind
  • Pandas have excellent camouflage for their habitat
